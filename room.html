ELEGANTLY REFINED SPACE JUST FOR YOU

Amidst the bustling city lies our luxurious room and suites that offers guests an exceptional home away from home. With 473 spacious guestrooms, our various room categories offer the utmost comfort, as well as convenience with great room amenities, perfect for business-savvy guests, solo travellers, couples and families alike.

Explore Our Rooms

DELUXE ROOM

Choose from a king bed or two twin beds and stay productive at the generous working space.Meanwhile, a rejuvenating session awaits at the large marble bathroom, complete with a separate shower and bathtub.

PREMIERE ROOM

Experience warm and relaxing earth tones in our Premier Room, which features a king-size or twin-size bed, a large marble bathroom with a separate shower and a generous working space outfitted with high-tech enhancements.

GRAND PREMIERE ROOM

Detailed with earthy tones and plush fabrics to strike a balance between elegance as well as comfort, sink into the comfort of our super king-size or twin-size beds.

THE L-CLUB ROOM

With dedicated floors on higher levels, our L-Club rooms offer guests picturesque city view and access to personalised services at our exclusive L-Club.

Explore Our Suites

EXECUTIVE SUITE

Choose from nine suites that offer a spacious master bedroom furnished with a king bed, a living area and a marble bathroom.

AMBASSADOR SUITES

Located on higher floors, Tasik Hotel's Ambassador Suites offer a breathtaking view of the city. This hotel suite features a master bedroom as well as a separate living and dining area.

PRESIDENTIAL SUITES

Witness unrivalled city skyline views in one of our most distinguished suite that features a separate living room, dining area, a lush king bed and more grand amenities.

ROYAL SUITES

Known to host various prominent names, the Royal Suite represents the highest level of luxury in Tasik Hotel with spacious rooms built to offer the paramount of comfort and capture unrivalled city views.
